November 2025 – Our Inaugural Issue (Open for Submissions)

We are excited to announce the launch of Faun by Moonlight's inaugural issue in November 2025. This milestone represents the realization of our goal to create a dedicated platform for outstanding literary fiction.

Current Status

To date, we have received numerous submissions from talented writers whose work exemplifies character-driven storytelling and sophisticated prose. The overall quality has been inspiring.

Submissions remain open as we continue to seek remarkable stories that resonate deeply with readers.

What to Expect

Our November issue will feature a curated selection of literary fiction chosen for its technical excellence, emotional depth, and unique voice. We look for stories that engage intelligent readers and reward careful attention with profound insights.

Submission Window

Submissions will remain open for our inaugural and all subsequent issues. We encourage writers whose work aligns with our vision to submit their writing. Our editorial team is committed to giving each submission the thoughtful consideration it deserves.

For detailed submission guidelines, please visit our Submissions page. We look forward to discovering new voices.
