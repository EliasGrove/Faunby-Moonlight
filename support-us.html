Support Us

Faun by Moonlight operates as a free-first publication. Our monthly issues, submissions process, editorial work, and reading access cost nothing. This approach reflects our belief that quality literary fiction should remain accessible.

Our publication runs on volunteer effort from individuals committed to finding and showcasing great literature. We maintain our standards through this collective dedication.

Ways to Support Our Work

Volunteer

Whether it's editorial work, website development, marketing, beta reading, or other publication tasks, we can always use another helping hand. If you have relevant skills or interest, we welcome your help. Feel free to reach out to us through our contact page.

Purchase Annual Collections

Every year we compile our strongest works into a curated yearly issue. These collections will include exclusive content, artwork, opinion pieces and more, providing a way to support the publication and the authors directly.

Buy Us a Coffee

If you'd like to fuel our late-night editing sessions, our Buy Me a Coffee page helps keep us caffeinated. But don't feel obligated..

Share Our Content

Recommend stories to other readers, engage with our social media posts, direct writers to our submission guidelines, or discuss our published work. Organic promotion through readers remains essential for literary publications.

Your readership itself constitutes support. Each person who engages seriously with the work we publish advances our central purpose: bringing you great literature.
