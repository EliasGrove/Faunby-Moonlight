Submission Information

Faun by Moonlight accepts unagented literary fiction submissions. Submit short stories and novels up to 10,000 words. No submission fees. Response within 2 weeks. We seek character-driven fiction with psychological depth and literary merit.

What We're Looking For

We seek literary fiction that exhibits artistic merit, complex characterization, and emotional resonance. Literary fiction emphasizes character-driven narratives, psychological insight, and thematic richness over plot-centric storytelling.

Submission Guidelines

  • Format: Standard manuscript format (double-spaced, 12-point Times New Roman, 1-inch margins)
  • Length: Up to 10,000 words
  • File Type: Microsoft Word (.docx) only
  • Email submissions to: submissions@faunbymoonlight.com
  • Response time: Within 2 weeks
  • No submission fees
  • Simultaneous submissions accepted
  • Unagented submissions preferred

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept unagented submissions?
Yes, we prefer unagented submissions. We are committed to discovering and nurturing new voices directly.
Do you pay contributors?
Currently unable to offer payment for individual submissions, but we provide quick response times and plan annual anthology with compensation.
How quickly do you respond?
We aim to respond to all submissions within two weeks.
Do I have to pay to submit?
No. There is no fee to submit your work to Faun by Moonlight.

About Faun by Moonlight

Faun by Moonlight is an online literary publication dedicated to showcasing exceptional literary fiction. We believe this genre offers profound insights into human experience through its exploration of psychological depth, social commentary, and artistic vision.
